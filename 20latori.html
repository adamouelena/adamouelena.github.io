20LATORI PORTATILI

Italian definition> ventilatore = fan, ventilator / 20=venti / latore=bearer  ￼20 portable bearers

Video installation (on loop)

Twenty separate windows reproduce the movement of a ceiling fan taken from different angles and in different speeds. Redefining the potential of the element which turns from object to subject. 

The old ventilator continues on spinning without causing any effective changes in the surrounding air of the house. 
The obsessive observation focuses the attention on the lack of functionality.
