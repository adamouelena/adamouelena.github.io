RUNNING OUT OF

Embroidering-Time

Performance, 122', Spazio Arôme, Milan, November 2015

‘’ Riguardo al tempo, il climax, è quel momento in cui il tempo si innalza
talmente al di sopra che,  pur non essendo eternità, e come se lo fosse,
o in altri termini, essa è eternità, ma solo per un momento. ‘’ 


''Regarding time, climax, is that moment in which time rises up to such an extension that, even if not being eternity, feels like if it were, or in other terms, it is eternity, but only for a moment.'' 

Manlio Sgalambro, Italian philosopher and writer. 

Human enclosed in a hypothetical transparent bubble, in an ex temporal dimension of time.  A double renounce; to time as memory and to time as a fleeing element depending neither on reason  nor on a psychological or metaphysical factor.
