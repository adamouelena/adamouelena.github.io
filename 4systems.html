4 SYSTEMS

video pass: heimatfour

A Four-Screen Video Installation.

hd video, color, sound, 15'42'', 2015

￼ ''The piece can be reproduced either by a soloist or by an ensemble. The piece has neither specific score nor a permanent rapport between its parts.'' 
Four Systems by Earl Brown, lesson on the indeterminate composition method.

''A trace remains, and I return unexpectedly, weeks later, in a different scene.'' 
Peter Brook

4 Systems:
Identifying the significance of the word 'home', while such definition is abandoned at the same time.
Three presences and an absence coexist by standing on the limit of their sharing sonorous landscape. 
The four screens become holes of aperture to enable the continuous alternation of an in/out/around view of the house, its object-inhabitants and its surrounding environment. 
The formal similarities of the architectural structures become the only bond between these different spaces, which get fused together by the current distant memory. 

with: Varsia Adamou, Giulia Masci, Rocco Siliotto.
featuring track by Stefanos Papadopoulos
Shot in Cyprus, Milan, Niece, Passignano Sul Trasimeno, Rome, L’Aquila.
